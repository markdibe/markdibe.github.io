About me

First and foremost, I love writing code. Ever since writing my first program in C++ and manipulating it to stdout the famous hello world! I have been obsessed with the idea of knowing how things work and how these machines work with only 1’s and 0’s. Software engineering is a never-ending puzzle that I am passionately engaged in solving. I believe in the power of programming to transform and improve the lives of people around the world.

For my first contract, I have had the chance to start working in web application development as an intern while I am starting my second year in university. I was thriven to learn every day new things about programming and how things are written on my local computer can be saved into a database-server that existed in the United States. I believe that the weight of responsibility thrown on me doubled my experience and insistence on finding how to solve a problem. I have had the chance to develop a web application for internal-website management, an application for the visitors, Employee information management, RFID attendance, location-mapper for Beirut-summit, and many more! At the same time, I have had a part-time job offer to build a content management system for a funder that wants to create an e-learning solution that is especially focused on the MENA region! And now I am into creating automation solutions for Nexius-Yuvo and teaching a web-development course founded by USAID!

Outside the work, I love to help my developer friends when they feel stacked, as I spent time learning new technologies and frameworks. For the rest of my time, I spent it with my family and books!
